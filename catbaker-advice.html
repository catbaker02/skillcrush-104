Hi my name is Cat and I have some advice for you!

Be very patient
with yourself.

Learning to code takes time, so be patient with yourself. When you start on a new journey, there's a big learning curve. Realize you are not going to perfect all the coding skills overnight. Be very patient with yourself. It is okay if all tasks don't go as planned. This does not mean that you are failure... It just means that you are learning. Learning something new takes time.So you have to tell yourself to breathe and be patient.

A fresh perspective is sometimes the key.

When you are learning to code there are times when things are not working as you thought they would. Even when you try different ways to problem solve, it might continue to not go according to plan... It is important to realize that this can happen. It doesn't mean you are a failure. You are learning! When you feel like you are hitting a wall due to frustration, take a break. Your vision at the momement is clouded by frustration. A break will give you the fresh perspective that is needed to solve the problem.

Google is your
best friend

When you are trying to solve problems that vex you, look no further than GOOGLE. This is an invaluable resource. Google can help you find the solution to the problem you are encountering with code. It can also provide you with a resource that can help to enhance your understanding of a coding concept.
